## Who handles shipping

Every order placed via skellywags.club is fulfilled by [Fourthwall](https://fourthwall.com). They print, pack, and ship merch from their fulfillment partners. They also handle returns, exchanges, lost packages, and anything that goes wrong in transit.

## How shipping works

Skellywags merch is **print-on-demand** — it gets printed AFTER you order, then shipped. Typical timeline:

- **Production**: 2–7 business days (depends on the product)
- **Shipping**: 3–10 business days domestic US, longer international
- **Total**: usually 1–3 weeks door-to-door

You'll get a tracking number from Fourthwall once your order ships.

## Returns + exchanges

Fourthwall has a **satisfaction guarantee** — if your item arrives damaged, defective, or wrong, they reprint or refund. They cover most quality issues automatically.

For all returns, exchanges, and customer-service questions, contact Fourthwall directly:

- **Full Fourthwall returns + shipping policy**: see your Fourthwall order confirmation email for direct links, or visit the [storefront](https://skelly-xkh-shop.fourthwall.com).
- **Lost package?** Reach out via the order email — Fourthwall's support is responsive.

## Questions Skelly handles directly

If something's wrong with the merch design, the website, a coupon code, or anything brand-side (not shipping logistics), ping Skelly on [Discord](https://discord.gg/zpWv2cXxB9).
